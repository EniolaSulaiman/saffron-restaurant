Our Beginning

From a Dream to a Destination

Saffron Table was born in 2012 from a simple belief: that Mediterranean cooking — ancient, soulful, generous — deserved a space where it could breathe, evolve, and be celebrated.

Founders Amara and Daniel Khalil, both children of Lebanese and Italian immigrants, grew up watching their grandmothers transform humble ingredients into extraordinary meals. That memory became the seed of everything Saffron Table stands for today.

From our very first service — 12 covers, one borrowed table — to the 60-seat dining room and private events space we inhabit today, we've stayed true to the same conviction: great food is an act of love, and every guest deserves to feel that.

Meet the Chef

Chef Nadia Osman

Executive Chef Nadia Osman brings 20 years of culinary experience spanning Beirut, Lyon, and New York. Trained at Le Cordon Bleu Paris and mentored under three Michelin-starred chefs, she returned to her Mediterranean roots with a singular mission: to create food that feels like coming home.

Her cooking philosophy is built on restraint and respect — respecting the ingredient, respecting the season, and respecting the culture each dish represents. The result is cuisine that is both deeply rooted and thrillingly contemporary.
